Solution

After collecting all of the research, we were asked to determine the primary call-to-action of the home page, as well as develop a style tile.

Call to action

Style Tile

This is the style tile that I created for the updated ACPB website that features key elements of the site design such as type, CTA buttons, and color that communicate the mood of the organization.

My style tile displays the how I think the Angel City Pit Bulls website would best look and feel. I wanted to communicate how the website should feel when you are visiting it and overall how the organization is displayed.

The colour palette was the biggest thing to me because it is the foundation of how each element after that is going to look. I kept the yellow that ACPB already has for their logo and built off of that.

I want the viewers of the website to feel a call to donate to the organization, combining the logo with a call to action button for donation was an idea that became further developed.
