Design Challenge Page

Client Research

- Angel city pits is a LA based organization that started as a group of pit bull lovers wanted to let people know how they really are as a breed.

- They have multiple options on how to support. Fostering, donation and social media awareness. There are also volunteer options for locals.

- They mainly get information out via newsletter with an email sign up.

- They provide resources to those who want more information on topics like spay/neuter, rehoming your pet, and finding a stay.

- Their website also has a blog section that hasn’t been updated in a while, could also use an update.

Design Challeneg Questions

Design Challenge

What is the problem you are trying to solve?

- Inform people about the hardships pit bulls have faced as a breed and help these dogs find a forever home.

1. Frame it as a question:

- How can we show people that pit bulls are loving dogs and show them adoption opportunities?

2. Now, state the ultimate impact you’re trying to have.

- Give pit bulls in the Los Angles area a chance to find a home with people who understand the bad wrap that the breed has been given and will love them.

3. What are some possible solutions to your problem?

- Creating a more user friendly platform where people can get more information about the organization.

4. Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you’re facing.

- Having to overcome the thoughts that people have against pit bulls.

- Showcasing all the information in a clean yet effective way.

5. Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

- How can we overcome the thoughts people have against the pit bull breed and show that they are loving animals while showcasing the opprotunities of adoption, donation, and volunteer.
